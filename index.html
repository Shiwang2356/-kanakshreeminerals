High-Quality Muscovite Mica.

Premium-grade mica processed to stringent international standards.

Muscovite Mica is a high-quality, silvery, platy mineral belonging to the mica group. It is chemically inert, non-toxic, and highly resistant to heat and weathering. Its excellent cleavage allows it to split into thin, flexible sheets with a brilliant pearly luster. Our Muscovite Mica flakes are carefully processed and screened into various mesh sizes suitable for multiple industrial applications including oil-well drilling, paints, plastics, rubber, insulation materials, and more.

Industrial Grade Biotite Mica.

Iron-rich mica optimized for structural shielding and protective coatings.

Biotite, often referred to as 'Black Mica', is characterized by its significant iron and magnesium content. It offers exceptional UV protection and chemical stability, making it indispensable for high-performance industrial formulations.

Industrial Soda Feldspar.

Optimized sodium-aluminum silicate for glazes and glass manufacturing.

Soda Feldspar is characterized by its high $Na_2O$ content, making it an excellent flux for low-temperature industrial processes. It is vital for achieving high-gloss glazes and improving the clarity and chemical resistance of commercial glass products.
